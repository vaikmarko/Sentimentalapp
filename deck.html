PART 1: THE VISION & OPPORTUNITY

The Last Frontier of Human Experience

In a world increasingly defined by algorithms and artificial experiences...

Your authentic life story is the most deeply human treasure you possess.

We're building the platform that ensures it's preserved, understood, and shared in meaningful ways.

The Problem: Our Stories Are Being Lost

  • Fragmentation: Our lives are scattered across devices, platforms, and formats
  • Overwhelm: Too many moments, too little meaning
  • Skills Gap: Most people can't effectively tell their own stories
  • Lost Legacy: The next generation will inherit social feeds, not life stories
  • Format Mismatch: The same story doesn't work for all audiences
"Future generations will know what we looked like, but not who we were."

Current Solutions Fall Short
